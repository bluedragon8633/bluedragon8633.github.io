Irvington High School Renovation | June 29, 2022

Summary

First day of records. All trees in the largest parking lot, in front of the Maher Gym, and the High School atrium have been removed.

Notes

-An unusually high number of robins, starlings, and crows in the vicinity. Possibly displaced?

Images

Front of High School atrium. Stairs and surrounding trees have been removed; some small bushes remain.

Front of the library and Maher gym. All large bushes and small trees removed; the benches used to be very shady at midday. The same type of large bushes remain on the eastern side of the Maher Gym.

Main parking lot. All small trees have been completely removed, including the evergreen by the sidewalk.

More fences and barriers behind High School.
