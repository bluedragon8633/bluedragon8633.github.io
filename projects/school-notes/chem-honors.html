Equations

Properties

  • Physical Properties: measurable/observable with 5 senses
    • Includes dissolving, phase change
    • Usually reversible (ex. phase change); not always though (ex. lacerations)
    • Extensive physical properties: dependent on amount (ex. calories, mass, volume)
    • Intensive physical properties: dependent on type (ex. hardness, density, melting point)
  • Chemical Properties
    • Describe behavior/potential to undergo chemical change when exposed to other substances/energy
    • Usually irreversible
    • Only observable during chemical reactions
